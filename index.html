About Us

Vermax Consultation Limited is a growing firm dedicated to empowering SMEs with financial expertise. Founded by Maxwell Ojwang’, the firm was established to address the lack of best financial practices among businesses in Kenya. With deep knowledge in accounting and finance, Vermax helps businesses streamline operations and achieve sustainable growth.

  • Our Vision

    To be a trusted partner in financial consulting, empowering SMEs in Kenya to achieve sustainable growth and compliance.

  • Our Mission

    To provide expert financial solutions in accounting, tax, and business consulting, enabling SMEs to streamline operations and thrive in a competitive market

  • Our Core Values

    Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Client Focus, and Confidentiality.

  • Sectors We Serve

    Tourism, Manufacturing, Retail, Professional Services, and Startups—offering tailored financial solutions for compliance, growth, and efficiency.
